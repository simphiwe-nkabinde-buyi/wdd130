The Trusted Reviewers

Here is a list of reviewers that we trust. These reviewers have years of expereince under their belt and they've been around the block for a while. This is not necessarily an exhaustive list of good reviewer sites. If you know a good and credible organisation that publishes tech reviewers online then feel free to email us your list.

PCMag.com

PCMag.com is a leading authority on technology, delivering lab-based, independent reviews of the latest products and services. Our expert industry analysis and practical solutions help you make better buying decisions and get more from technology.For more than 40 years, rigorous, repeatable testing has been at the core of everything we do here at PCMag

GSMArena.com

GSMArena is an online website that offers comprehensive and up-to-date mobile phone information. GSMArena.com has as its primary task to provide detailed and accurate information about mobile phones and their features. On the official GSMArena.com YouTube channel you'll find hands-on videos, user interface walkthroughs, unboxings and many more videos related to the world of mobile technology.

Techradar.com

TechRadar is an online publication focused on technology, with editorial teams in the US, UK, Australia and India. It provides news and reviews of tech products and gadgets.Not only does TechRadar help you find the tech you'll love, they're passionate about tell you how to use it, how to make it work with other vital parts of your life and even what apps, games and media you should think about downloading, playing and watching.
